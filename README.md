# Expense Tracker - Your Personal Financial Buddy

**Whether you're tracking daily coffee runs or managing a strict monthly budget, Expense Tracker provides the insights you need in a sleek, intuitive interface.

---

## ✨ What Expense Tracker Can Do For You

### 📊 Understand Your Money at a Glance
*   **Smart Dashboard**: Instantly see your total balance and how much you've spent this month the moment you open the app.
*   **Visual Spending Trends**: Easy-to-read charts break down exactly where your money is going (e.g., Food, Travel, Entertainment).
*   **Recent Activity**: A clean, organized list of your most recent transactions right on the home screen.

### ⚡ Lightning-Fast Expense Logging
*   **Custom Numpad**: We built a custom, large-button keypad so you can enter transaction amounts in seconds with one hand.
*   **Smart Categorization**: Quickly assign expenses to main categories and subcategories with just a few taps.

### 🎯 Stay on Track with Budgets
*   **Custom Limits**: Set your own Daily, Weekly, and Monthly spending limits.
*   **Visual Safety Zones**: Adjust sliders to define your "Safe Haven," "Mild Caution," and "Danger" spending thresholds. The app will visually warn you when you're getting too close to your limits.

### 🎨 Personalize Your Experience
*   **Custom Categories**: Don't like the default categories? Create your own! Add custom subcategories, pick the perfect icon, and choose a unique accent color to match your style.
*   **Global Preferences**: Easily switch your base currency directly from the settings.

### 🔒 Secure & Private Cloud Backup
*   **Google Drive Integration**: Connect your Google account to securely and automatically back up your financial records to your personal Google Drive. 
*   **End-to-End Encryption**: Your data is encrypted, meaning only you have access to your financial history.
*   **Right to Delete**: Complete transparency and control over your data. You can permanently delete your account and wipe all associated records at any time.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

To get Expense Tracker running on your device:
1. Clone this repository to your local machine.
2. Ensure you have Flutter installed.
3. Run `flutter pub get` to fetch dependencies.
4. Run `flutter run` to launch the app on your emulator or physical device.
